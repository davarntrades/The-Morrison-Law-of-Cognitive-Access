If `∂C/∂I ≈ 0` and `C ⊥ L`, then no amount of information, argument, or linguistic intervention can restore hearing.

The only intervention that works is **direct topological perturbation** — an event or experience that acts on the C-axis directly, bypassing language entirely. The manifold must be physically deformed. Not persuaded. Not educated. Deformed.

This is why:

- Trauma can produce sudden insight (forced ΔG — the manifold had no choice but to reshape).

- Argument, education, and debate almost never produce deep change (all L-axis — zero C-axis projection).

The geometry predicts exactly which interventions work and which don’t — and why.
