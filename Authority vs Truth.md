## The Observation

A compelling idea from a credentialed source gets hundreds of reactions.

A more precise correction of that idea — from someone less known — gets ten.

```

Same thread.
One is the observation, dressed in authority.
One is the actual explanation.

Hundreds vs ten.

Not because hundreds of people
understood the first post better.
Because the first post came from someone
whose topology they already trust.

The reactions are not to the content.
They are to the signal.

"This person seems credible.
 This seems important.
 I will signal that I agree."
```

This is not stupidity. It is not intellectual laziness. It is the Morrison Law of Cognitive Access™ operating in real time — and it has a precise geometric explanation.

```

  without requiring topology to change.

  Not to the content.
  To the signal.
```
